Key differences

  • Opponents strip — all 3 opponents in a single horizontal row at the top. Small avatar (tap to expand video). Card count shown as mini backs. Current turn highlighted in gold.
  • Full-width trick area — center gets more vertical space since the side columns are gone.
  • Full-width bidding bar — Order Up / Pass spans the whole screen width, easy to tap without mis-tapping.
  • Larger hand cards — 24px wide vs 16px currently. Still fits 5 cards comfortably.
  • Self-video compact — small row above your hand. Camera/mute buttons inline. No wasted space.
  • Score + trump in header — always visible, never hidden behind other content.
